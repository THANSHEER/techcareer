About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2015, TechCareer has been dedicated to bridging the gap between education and employment in the tech industry. We started with a small team passionate about nurturing tech talent.

Today, we've trained over 10,000 students with a placement success rate of 95% in leading tech companies across the globe.

Our Mission

To empower individuals with industry-relevant skills and connect them with meaningful career opportunities in technology.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted platform for tech education and placement services, enabling careers that shape the future of technology.
